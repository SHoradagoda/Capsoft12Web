Calypso Upgrade

Calypso is a front, back and middle office software that's provide a complex set of tasks for the business. We engaged in a long term project with a leading bank to migrate their Calypso implementation which handled FX and Commodity trading. The primary task was to ensure that the Middle office reports aligned in both versions. This included reports that produced first and even second order greeks. The project was delivered successfully.
